About Me

My purpose is to make websites that are easy to read, access and use. As we move towards a future that is becoming more and more dependant on computers and cellphones, my goal is to be able to create websites that everyone can effectively use and understand. I look forward to working with other folks who believe that websites can be fun, educational, accessible and magical.

What a beautiful thing it is to mix, combine and add a bunch of seasonings or code and come up with a delicious meal, or easy to read website!
